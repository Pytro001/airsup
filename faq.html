Airsup FAQ: answers for suppliers and customers

Buyer and founder questions

What is Airsup?›
Airsup connects founders and buyers, primarily from Europe and the US, with vetted, high-quality manufacturers in China. It is optimized for the customer side, unlike Alibaba which is built for suppliers.
How is this different from Alibaba?›
Alibaba relies on self-service search and AI chat, which makes quality hard to predict. Airsup manually screens factories and uses AI to accurately match your project to the right manufacturer.
Who can currently use the platform?›
Right now access is limited to a selected group of high quality startups and suppliers, to help improve the platform safely. A broader rollout to more customers is planned for end of May 2026.
Does Airsup handle the transaction?›
No. Airsup is a matching platform. Once you are connected with a supplier, all negotiation, ordering, and payment is handled directly between you and the factory.

Factory and supplier questions

Is it free to join as a supplier?›
Yes, completely free. No entry fees, no commissions, and no hidden charges. If that ever changes, you will be notified in advance and have the right to leave before any costs apply.
How do I receive customer inquiries?›
You get notified via the Airsup dashboard when a customer project matches your profile. You can then accept or decline the inquiry and connect directly with the customer via WhatsApp or WeChat.
What do I need to provide to get started?›
Just basic company info: production capacity, budget range, minimum order quantity, and a contact person. No product photos or price lists required upfront; it is all handled during onboarding.
Who handles payments and disputes?›
All payment terms, transactions, and dispute resolution are agreed directly between you and the customer. Airsup does not process payments or take responsibility for financial disputes.
Does Airsup allocate inquiries by category or region?›
Currently, matching is based on what works best for both sides. The exact method may evolve over time to whatever produces the fastest, most accurate matches.
