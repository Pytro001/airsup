How it works

1
Describe what you need
Tell us what you're building: product type, materials, quantities, target regions. Upload drawings or files if you have them.
2
We find matched suppliers
We search manufacturers across China, the US, and Europe and match your brief to the right capabilities and capacity.
3
Your brief is sent to suppliers
Matched suppliers receive your project brief directly. No need to cold-contact dozens of factories yourself.
4
Supplier reviews and responds
Each supplier reviews your request and either accepts, declines, or asks clarifying questions. You don't need to chase anyone.
5
Contact handed over once aligned
When both sides are aligned, we hand over direct contact details. From there, you move fast with no middlemen in the conversation.
