How it works

From idea to supplier contact in a few steps.

1
Describe what you need
Tell us what you're building — product type, materials, quantities, target regions. Upload drawings or files if you have them.
2
AI finds matched suppliers
Our AI searches vetted manufacturers across China, the US, and Europe — matching your brief to the right capabilities and capacity.
3
Your brief is sent to suppliers
Matched suppliers receive your project brief directly. No need to cold-contact dozens of factories yourself.
4
Supplier reviews and responds
Each supplier reviews your request and either accepts, declines, or asks clarifying questions — on their end, not yours.
5
Contact handed over once aligned
When both sides are aligned, we hand over direct contact details. From there, you move fast — no middlemen in the conversation.
