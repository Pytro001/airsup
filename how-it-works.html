How it Works

1
Describe what you need
Tell us what you're building: product type, materials, quantities, target manufacturing region. Upload drawings or files if you have them.
2
We find suppliers
We search manufacturers across China, the US, and Europe and contact the best ones based on your project requirements.
3
Supplier review
The supplier reviews your request and either accepts, declines, or asks clarifying questions. When they accept, we hand you their direct contact.
4
Contact
Once connected, you negotiate terms, samples, and pricing directly with the manufacturer. No middlemen, no markups.
5
Towards production
Upgrade your plan anytime for on-site QA and a dedicated manager.
