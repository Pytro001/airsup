For Suppliers

What is Airsup?
Airsup connects global buyers with the best manufacturers. We handle the matching. You focus on production.
How does it work?
We research and identify the best factories. We found you.
You sign up and complete your factory profile
We visit your factory to verify quality and production standards
When a customer signs up with a project that fits your factory, our agent Supi sends you a WhatsApp message with their requirements. If you have questions about the project ask Supi, he will know.
When you accept the project, we hand our customer your WhatsApp and they will write you directly
How much do we need to pay?
Nothing. Joining and using Airsup is completely free for suppliers. No costs, no commissions. We charge customers for getting access to our supplier network.
What we need from you
A contact person who speaks English, responds fast, and is always reachable on WhatsApp
Openness to factory visits by customers
Permission to film production videos for customers

By signing up you accept these terms.

What you get
New international customers based on your quality and speed. No marketing needed.
Direct contact with buyers, no middlemen
